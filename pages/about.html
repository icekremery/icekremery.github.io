About Me

Growing up, I've always loved technology. Everything about how the internet works, electronics function, connect, and connect with people has kept me striving to learn more about technology.

Currently I am a sophomore at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, studying to get my Bachelors Degree in Information Technology and Web Science.

I love challenging myself and learning new things like languages or software. I love toying around with electronics like my Raspberry Pi, as well as delving into creative outlets like video production and video game creation.

My biggest value is that I believe that if you're going to do something, you should always do your best and nothing less.
